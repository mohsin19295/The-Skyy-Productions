About Us

    We are leading Music and Video Production company based out of Delhi. We are speaclized in audio, video, mixing and mastering. Catering to all kinds of genres all across India.

    We are behind the viral 'Ramanyan Rap' which crossed 1 million views.

    We have worked for various brands like Itel, Raksha4u, dabur and many more. We have worked with bollywood legend B Sanjh for a beautiful creation 'Mere Ilahi Vey'.

    We offer Diploma and certification courses for students all across India along with placement assistance.
